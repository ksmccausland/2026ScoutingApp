Team Ball Comparison

Load a published Google Sheets CSV, choose up to three teams from the Team # column, and compare their average Auto Balls, Teleop Balls, and Total Balls in a stacked vertical bar chart.

Required sheet columns: Team #, Auto Balls, and Teleop Balls. For Google Sheets, a stable CSV URL usually looks like: .../gviz/tq?tqx=out:csv or .../export?format=csv.
